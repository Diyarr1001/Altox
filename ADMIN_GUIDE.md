# Admin Access Guide - Altox Labs LLP

This guide explains how to access the administrative dashboard of the Altox Labs website.

## How to Login

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**:
   Open your browser and go to `[your-domain].com/login` (or `http://localhost:3000/login` in development).

2. **Enter Credentials**:
   Use your administrative credentials to sign in.
   *Note: For the current development phase, use the demo credentials provided by the development team.*

3. **Access the Dashboard**:
   Upon successful login, you will be automatically redirected to the `/admin` dashboard.

## Administrative Dashboard Features

- **Site Overview**: Summary of active services and molecules.
- **Inquiry Management**: View and respond to client inquiries received through the contact form.
- **Content Updates**: Links to documentation and data structures for managing site content.

---

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Keep your login credentials secure. Unauthorized access to the admin panel can lead to unintended site changes.
